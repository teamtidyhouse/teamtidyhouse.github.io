Meet Cyndra Harris, owner of team tidy house

After earning a degree in Interior Design, and working in that field, I've taken my organizing skills to a new level. 

I learned how to implement space planning and design principles, while also taking into consideration the lifestyles of the homeowner. 

I always ask "What is most important to YOU?" 

experience

I have over 10 years of practice and paid organizing. At every job I've ever had, and home I've lived in, I would organize everything I could get my hands on. As a teen, babysitting was the best, because I could sort their toys. Organizing has just always been my hobby and passion.

favorite Organizing tip

I took all of my DVDs and CDs out of their cases and put them into 2 large white binders. They are so sleek and so much neater than chaotic shelves full of cases. I may not even use these practically obsolete items anymore, but if you can't part with them, like me, this is a great solution!
